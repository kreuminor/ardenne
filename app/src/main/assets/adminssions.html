Ardenne Preparatory School accepts applications for admission on a rolling basis throughout the year. While there is no admissions deadline, we encourage you to register your child/ward as soon as possible to ensure the availability of space. School fees are determined on an annual basis after the budgeting process has been completed. Fees can be paid (using vouchers obtained from the bursary) at any branch of NCB or at the school using a debit or credit card.

Parents/Guardians who are interested in having their child/ward enrolled at are kindly asked to take note of the following documents which are required to complete the registration process:

  • One Passport-Sized Photograph of the child

  • Copy of Child’s Birth Certificate

  • Copy of Immunization Card

  • Copy of Last School’s Report and Recommendation from Previous School ( for students entering Grade 1 – 6)

  • Registration Fee of One Thousand Dollars (Non-refundable)
